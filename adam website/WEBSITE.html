REST IN PEACE AKIRA

Akira Toriyama, the visionary creator of Dragon Ball, has left an indelible mark on the world of manga and anime. His boundless imagination and unparalleled storytelling brought to life iconic characters such as Goku, Vegeta, and Piccolo, captivating audiences of all ages.

With Dragon Ball, Toriyama-sensei not only entertained but also inspired generations, teaching valuable lessons of friendship, perseverance, and the enduring power of hope. His impact on popular culture is immeasurable, and his spirit will continue to guide and inspire creators and fans alike.

Though you may have departed from this world, your legendary legacy will continue to transcend time and space, echoing across the cosmos like the Kamehameha wave, forever etched in the hearts of those whose lives you touched.

Rest in peace, Akira Toriyama, and thank you for sharing your extraordinary talents with the world. Your Dragon Balls will grant eternal wishes in the realm of our imagination.
